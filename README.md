# Melody Transposer
(This was made with Copilot which is why it sounds funny lol)
Bring your melodies to life in a new key with just a few clicks.

Melody Transposer is a lightweight web app designed for musicians, students, and hobbyists who want to quickly transpose a melody between keys while preserving its musical feel. Whether you are exploring new tonal centers, preparing arrangements, or practicing theory, this tool makes the process simple and immediate.

## What it does

Enter a melody, choose its original key and mode, pick a target key and mode, and the app will generate a transposed version for you. The interface is intentionally simple so you can focus on the music rather than the mechanics.

## Features

- Transpose melodies between different keys
- Switch between major and minor modes
- Work directly in the browser with no setup required
- Keep the experience lightweight and easy to use

## How to use it

1. Open the app in your browser by launching [index.html](index.html).
2. Enter the melody as space-separated notes, such as: C D E F G.
3. Type the original key and choose the original mode.
4. Enter the new key and choose the target mode.
5. Click Transpose to see the result.

## Example

If you start with a melody in C major and transpose it to A minor, the app will adjust the notes accordingly so the melody remains musically coherent in the new key.
